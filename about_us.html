Welcome to the Hive, Carl Wunsche Sr. High School's very own business and career program! Student-led in a career-based academy, our mission is to help students develop strong career and technical skills that prepares them for postsecondary education or the workforce.

Created as a business and marketing idea for Wunsche's FBLA in 2021, the Hive developed into a program that supplements the career and technical education provided by Carl Wunsche Sr. High School. We found that most students struggled on deciding their major after high school due to the lack of opportunities to diversify their interests and find what they are passionate about. This program allows students to have more hands-on experience with their career pathway, while also letting them have fun and encouraging their passions in the process!

The Hive offers many career and technical programs:

  • Engineering
  • Robotics
  • Computer Programming
  • Data Science
  • Information Systems
  • Business
  • Finance
  • Public Speaking
